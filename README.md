 Stokka — Desktop Inventory Management for Small Shops                                                                                                                                                                     
                                                                                                                                                                                                                            
  Stokka is a simple, offline-first desktop application that helps small shop owners manage their inventory, purchases, and sales — no internet connection, no monthly fees, no complexity.                                 
                                                                                                                                                                                                                            
  What it does
                                                                                                                                                                                                                            
  - Products — track stock levels, get low-stock alerts, manage pricing and profit margins                                                                                                                                  
  - Purchase Orders — record supplier orders, mark them received, and watch stock update automatically
  - Sales Invoices — create invoices, apply discounts and VAT, accept partial payments, and track outstanding balances                                                                                                      
  - Customers & Suppliers — manage contacts and view their full transaction history                                                                                                                                         
  - Reports — sales, pending payments, inventory, profit & loss, customer and supplier statements                                                                                                                           
  - Receipts — print thermal receipts and A4 invoices directly from the app                                                                                                                                                 
                                                                                                                                                                                                                            
  Key features                                                                                                                                                                                                              
                                                                                                                                                                                                                            
  - Works 100% offline — all data stored locally in SQLite                                                                                                                                                                  
  - Bilingual interface: English and Arabic (full RTL support)
  - Hardware-bound licensing with a 7-day free trial                                                                                                                                                                        
  - Automatic updates for active maintenance subscribers                                                                                                                                                                    
                                                                                                                                                                                                                            
  Installation                                                                                                                                                                                                              
                                                                                                                                                                                                                            
  Download the latest .exe installer from the Releases page and run it. No additional software required.
